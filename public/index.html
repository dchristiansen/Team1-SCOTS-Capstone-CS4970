Welcome to RAC Trainer!

Rhythmic Auditory Cueing (RAC) is a form of rehabilitation that utilizes a recurring beat or rhythm to offer an external cue to influence motor skills, especially gait

Similar to how a metronome helps a musician stay on tempo, rhythmic stimulation provides an effective approach to improving gait parameters

RAC Trainer is a website that allows you to conduct interval RAC Training from wherever you are
